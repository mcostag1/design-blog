"Don't Forget" Box Models

Display Box

For this model, we wanted to focus on the mechanism of the lid. We were inspired by the 3D printed box with the threaded lid that iis in Doug Willen's office. We wanted a similar opening experience, but we wanted the lid to then lock into place once it reached a fully open state so that it could function as a display box. We pictured it housing a form of jewlery - a ring, necklace, watch, etc. - something that is worn every day. The user could place the item of jewlery in the display state when they are not wearing it, and close the box when they are wearing it. This will act as a visual reminder for them to put on the item of jewlery.

Harambe

We took the prompt "never forget" a little bit more figuratively with this model. We wanted to use an image that was very representative of something you should "never forget". In this case it took the form of Harambe, which transformed into a well-known meme that is often paired with the phrase "never forget". We thought it was an interesting way to pair the figurative language associated with the image with the physical idea of not forgetting very basic items, like keys, phone, wallet, etc.
